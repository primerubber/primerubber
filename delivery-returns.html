Delivery & Returns

UK Delivery Information

At Prime Rubber UK, we understand that getting your rubber products quickly is vital for your projects. We offer standard and express delivery options across the mainland United Kingdom.

Standard Delivery: 3-5 working days for in-stock items.

Express Delivery: 1-2 working days (available on selected products).

Hassle-Free Returns

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, you can return it to us within 14 days of delivery.

Standard Return Policy

  • Items must be in their original, unused condition.
  • Custom-cut rubber sheets or bespoke matting are non-returnable unless faulty.
  • Returns must be accompanied by the original delivery note.
  • The customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is damaged or incorrect.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your item arrives damaged or you receive the wrong product, please contact us immediately at sales@primerubber.co.uk or call 01282 778899. we will arrange for a replacement or a full refund including shipping costs.
