About Prime Rubber

The Legacy of British Resilience

Prime Rubber UK is more than a supplier—we are the definitive architects of industrial endurance. Since 2002, we have served the nation's most demanding sectors, from aerospace hubs to critical national infrastructure. Our mission is to engineer the unbreakable foundation that powers UK business.

Every product in our inventory is over-engineered for zero-failure performance, ensuring that your facility remains safe, compliant, and operational in environments that destroy ordinary materials.

Unrivaled Scale & Expertise

With 24+ years of polymer science expertise and the UK's largest live inventory of SBR, Neoprene, and Nitrile, we offer a technical scale that remains unchallenged. We don't just supply sheets; we deploy strategic industrial solutions tailored to the highest safety and performance standards.

The Prime Distinction

  • Bespoke Precision: Industrial rolls and sheets cut to the exact millimetre of your project requirements.
  • Mission-Critical Logistics: 24-hour rapid deployment protocol across the entire UK.
  • Total Compliance: 100% ISO certified, safety-tested, and built for Industrial Immortality.
